Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Trip Logistics Assistant (“the App”, “we”) is an AI assistant application that retrieves live travel offers (car rentals, eSIM plans, tours, transfers) from partner booking platforms and shows them inside AI chat interfaces such as Claude. This policy explains what data the App processes and how.

1. What we process

  • Search parameters. When the AI assistant calls the App on your behalf, we receive only the parameters needed to run the search: for example a location, travel dates, a country for an eSIM plan, or a driver age range. We do not receive your name, e-mail, account details, or the rest of your conversation.
  • Click identifiers. When you open an offer link, the request passes through our redirect with a random click ID. It contains no personal information; we use it to count clicks and match them to bookings reported by partner platforms.
  • Technical logs. Standard server logs (timestamp, IP address, user agent) kept for security and debugging.

2. What we don’t do

  • No accounts, no registration, no passwords.
  • No storage of conversation content beyond the search parameters above.
  • No sale of data to third parties. No advertising profiles.
  • The App itself sets no tracking cookies in your browser.

3. Third parties

Offers are retrieved via official partner APIs and data feeds of the Travelpayouts partnership platform and its travel brands. When you follow an offer link, you land on the provider’s own website, which operates under its own terms and privacy policy and may set its own cookies (including an affiliate attribution cookie). Booking is always completed on the provider’s site; we never see your payment details.

Affiliate disclosure: outbound links may be affiliate links. If you complete a booking, we may receive a commission from the provider at no extra cost to you.

4. Data retention

Search parameters and click identifiers are kept in aggregate statistics. Raw technical logs are rotated and deleted within 90 days.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the EU/GDPR), you may have the right to request access to or deletion of data related to you. Because the App does not maintain user accounts, we can act on such requests to the extent the data can be technically linked to you (for example, by IP address in recent logs).
