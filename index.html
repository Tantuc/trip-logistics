How it works

You ask in plain language

“eSIM for Turkey, 10 days”, “transfer from Bangkok airport”, “car in Tenerife next weekend” — right in your AI chat, no forms.

The assistant fetches live data

The app queries partner booking platforms for your exact parameters — dates, location, options — and returns a short comparison. This is data that generic web search can’t reach: it lives behind search forms.

You book at the source

Every offer links straight to the provider’s own site. Same price as going there directly — you just skip the searching part.

For partners & reviewers

Trip Logistics Assistant is an independent application built on the Model Context Protocol (MCP). It runs as a connector for Claude, with other assistant platforms planned. Traffic is organic user intent inside the chat: no paid search, no brand bidding, no iframes, no scraping — offers are retrieved via official partner APIs and data feeds, and every result links out to the provider’s own website.

Affiliate disclosure: outbound links may be affiliate links. If you book through them, we may earn a commission from the provider — at no extra cost to you. Prices are identical to booking directly.
